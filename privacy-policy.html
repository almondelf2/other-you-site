01 — Overview

The Short Version

Other You is built for couples. We collect only what's needed to make the app work, we never sell your data, and your personas and date history belong to you.

This Privacy Policy describes how Other You ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Other You: Multiverse Date Night web application (the "App") and our website at other-you.app.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

02 — Information We Collect

What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the Other You experience:

Profile Information (stored on your device)

  • Your first name and an optional one-line bio, entered during setup
  • These are stored in your browser's local storage only — they are never uploaded to any server in identifiable form
  • A randomly generated pair ID is created when you connect with a partner; it contains no personal information and is the only identifier the sync server ever sees

App Content You Create

  • Persona profiles: the alternate-life details you enter (career paths, cities, life choices)
  • Match history: records of which personas were paired and the generated date scenarios
  • Date completion notes and timestamps you add

Technical Information

  • Basic usage data (feature interactions, error logs) used solely for app improvement
  • Device type and browser version for compatibility purposes

We do not collect payment information directly — any future subscription billing is handled entirely by our payment processor.

03 — How We Use It

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account and couple pairing
  • Store and display your persona profiles and date history
  • Generate date scenario content through the match spin feature
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Respond to support requests you initiate
  • Send essential service communications (e.g., account changes, policy updates)

We do not use your persona content or date history for advertising, marketing profiling, or training AI models.

04 — Data Sharing

Who Can See Your Data

Your partner: Because Other You is designed for couples, certain app features are intentionally shared between paired accounts — specifically, the outcome of match spins and date history you both create together. Persona profile details remain private to each partner until a match spin reveals them.

Service providers: We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure provider for authentication and data storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf under a data processing agreement and does not use your data for its own purposes.

No selling: We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party, ever.

Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Other You or our users.

05 — Data Storage & Security

How We Protect Your Data

There are no user accounts in the traditional sense. Your name, bio, and personas are stored in your browser's local storage. When syncing with a partner, data is encrypted on your device before transmission using AES-256-GCM. The sync server (Supabase) only ever receives your randomly generated pair ID and an encrypted data blob it cannot read.

The Other You application is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and served over HTTPS.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no internet service is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

Data retention: We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data and app content within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

06 — Couples & Shared Data

A Note on Couple Pairing

Other You is designed to be used by two consenting adults in a relationship. Both partners should read this policy and agree to use the app together.

When two accounts are paired, certain data is shared as a core part of how the App functions:

  • Match spin results are visible to both partners
  • Shared date history is accessible to both partners
  • Each partner's individual persona details remain private until revealed by a spin

If you unpair or delete your account, your partner will no longer have access to your persona data, and shared date history may be removed.

07 — Your Rights

Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information in your account
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your persona and date history data (upon request)
  • Withdraw consent by discontinuing use of the App

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA. We honor those rights regardless of your location.

08 — Children's Privacy

Age Requirement

Other You is intended for adults aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a minor has created an account, we will delete that account promptly.

If you believe a minor is using the App, please contact us immediately.

09 — Changes

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the App evolves. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you by email or in-app notice.

Continued use of the App after a policy update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
