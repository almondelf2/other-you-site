Multiverse Date Night

Other You

Somewhere in the multiverse, all the versions of you are waiting to meet all the versions of them. This is a private app for couples to go on dates as their alternate selves — the roads not taken, the versions that were forgotten.

Enter the App Learn more ↓

What is Other You?

A deeper kind
of knowing

The slight deviation of a persona gives opportunity to explore yourself and your relationship. Ask deeper questions and share parts of yourself that don't come up in the daily routine.

🎭

Your alternate selves

Think of a crossroads in your life — a choice you didn't take, a phase that ended too soon. Create a persona for that version of you. Nickname, origin story, personality traits, character tags, and a photo so you can dress up for your date.
🎰

Personas get matched

The app pairs one of your alternate selves with one of theirs. Spin until the combination feels right, then accept and go on a date. Every pairing tells a different story.
💬

Explore new conversations

Most couples know each other well. But do they know the version who almost became a musician? The one who got arrested at a protest in college? The one who still writes poetry at midnight?
🕰

Date history

Every completed date is saved. Browse through which versions of yourselves have met, and when — a record of the alternate universe you've built together.

How it works

Three steps
into the multiverse

01

Build your other yous

Create personas for the versions of yourself defined by crossroads you didn't take. Your goth phase that never ended. The year you almost moved to Berlin. The artist you shelved for a stable job. Give them an alias, a story, a personality.
02

Connect with your partner

One partner generates a private code in the Connect tab and shares it over text or any messaging app. The other enters it in their app. That's it — no accounts, no links, no QR codes. Just a code.
03

Go on a multiverse date

Spin for a match. Two personas meet for the first time. Lock the slots you like and re-spin the rest. Accept when the combination feels right — then go on a date as those two versions of yourselves.

No App Store needed

Add it to your
home screen

Other You is a progressive web app — it installs directly from your browser in seconds, works offline, and feels completely native.

iPhone & iPad
  1. Open Safari and go to pwa.other-you.app
  2. Tap the Share button ⎙ at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add in the top right

Must use Safari — Chrome on iOS does not support installation.

Android
  1. Open Chrome and go to pwa.other-you.app
  2. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮ in the top right
  3. Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
  4. Tap Install to confirm

Chrome may show an automatic install banner — tap it to install in one tap.

Once installed, Other You opens full-screen like any native app — and works offline. No updates to download. No App Store account needed.
