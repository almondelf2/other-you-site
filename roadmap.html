Building in the open

The Roadmap

What's been built and where we're going.

"Most couples know each other's routines. Other You is about knowing each other's depth — the buried paths, the forgotten chapters, the potential versions."

The long-term goal is to become an intimate app a couple can share to strengthen their relationship and trigger deep conversation.

01
Completed

A Working App for Real Couples

Everything required for two real people to actually use Other You together. This phase ships as one complete release.

Persona creation

  • Persona profiles: alias, origin story, short descriptive bio, trait tags, emoji or photo
  • Reorder and delete personas

Date matching

  • Slot machine-like spin matching two personas for a date
  • Lock individual slots and re-spin the rest
  • Manual scroll through personas
  • Accept → Complete flow with date history
  • Shared canonical match result synced to both partners

Partner connection

  • Private code pairing: one partner generates a code, shares it via any messaging app, partner enters it to connect
  • End-to-end encrypted persona sync via Supabase
  • Offline queue: changes sync when both are online
  • Disconnect with partner notification

App foundation

  • Progressive Web App: installable from browser, works offline
  • Dark and light themes following device settings
  • Full WCAG AA accessibility compliance
  • Encrypted automatic backup with password protection and background file saving
  • Full demo mode with example personas and history
  • Date history with completed date tracking and editing
02
In Progress

Inspirational Prompts

Moving beyond basic persona spins with the addition of inspirational date prompts.
  • Personas creation prompts to combat writer's block
  • Date suggestions including themes, activities, and questions for deeper conversation
  • Journal feature added to history with reflection prompts and the recording of date theme details
  • Multi-device support: set up the app on a second device using your backup file
03
Planned

Persona Compatibility

Automated personalization and expanded date idea library.
  • Trait compatibility algorithm for tension and resonance between personas including date suggestions and questions for conversation
  • No-repeat matching: all persona combinations tried before any are repeated
04
Planned

AI Personalization

AI features add extremely personalized assistance and date recommendations.
  • Personalized AI features: date suggestions, questions, personas assistant
  • Fan fiction universes: not cosplay, but persona imagination
  • Real time cloud sync and backup via server
  • Personas can message each other
